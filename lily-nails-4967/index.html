About the salon

FAQ

Common questions before calling.

Do I need an appointment?

Appointments are best for gel sets and pedicures. Call ahead to confirm the next open chair.

How long does a gel manicure take?

Plan for about 45 to 60 minutes depending on removal, nail condition, and finish.

Can you remove existing gel?

Yes. Add removal time when booking so the technician can protect the natural nail.

Do you offer subtle nail art?

Yes. Bring a reference image and confirm the price before the service starts.
