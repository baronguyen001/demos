FAQ

Common questions before calling.

Can you help with same-day rush work?

Same-day help may be possible for simple fixes depending on workload. Call first before bringing an urgent piece.

Is there a minimum charge?

Small repairs may have a minimum fee. Confirm the quote before work starts.

Do you repair leather or suede?

Bring the item in for inspection. Some materials need specialist handling or may not be suitable.

What should I bring for fitting?

Bring the shoes, shirt, or layers you plan to wear with the garment so the fit can be pinned accurately.
